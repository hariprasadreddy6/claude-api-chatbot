RAG = Retrieval Augmented Generation

Purpose:
- work with large documents
- retrieve only relevant information

RAG workflow:
1. Split documents into chunks
2. User asks question
3. Search relevant chunks
4. Send relevant chunks to Claude
5. Claude generates answer

Benefits:
- faster
- cheaper
- scalable
- better accuracy

Used in:
- AI PDF chat
- enterprise AI
- document Q&A
- AI search systems
